Our Projects

Discover how Togetherness support is making a tangible difference in communities.

Quran Donation

The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said, "The best of you are those who learn the Qur'an and teach it." Our Quran Donation project aims to facilitate this noble act by providing copies of the Holy Quran to mosques, Islamic schools, and individuals who cannot afford one. Your donation helps spread the divine message and earns you immense reward.

Islamic Books Donations

Knowledge is a cornerstone of Islam. This project focuses on distributing a wide range of Islamic books, from the Seerah of the Prophet (ﷺ) to Fiqh and Tafsir, to libraries and communities. By enhancing Islamic literacy, we empower individuals to deepen their faith and understanding. Support this cause to be a part of spreading beneficial knowledge.

HASANAAT ORGANIZATION Food Drive

"He is not a believer who eats his fill while his neighbor beside him is hungry." Inspired by this teaching, our food drives provide essential sustenance to families, orphans, and the elderly. We distribute food packages and organize community kitchens to combat hunger and ensure no one in our community goes to bed hungry.

Ghusl Room Building

Fulfilling the final rights of a Muslim is a communal obligation (Fard Kifayah). This project involves constructing and maintaining dedicated Ghusl facilities for the ritual washing of the deceased. By contributing, you help ensure our brothers and sisters are given a dignified and Islamically-compliant final farewell.

Donate to this Project

Prison Visit

Islam teaches compassion and the possibility of redemption. Our prison visit program provides spiritual counseling, Islamic education, and moral support to inmates. We aim to foster rehabilitation and help individuals reconnect with their faith, preparing them for a positive reintegration into society upon release.

Donate to this Project

Tiling of Imam House

Our Imams are pillars of our community, providing guidance and leadership. This project focuses on improving the living conditions of our Imams by undertaking necessary renovations and repairs, such as tiling their homes. Supporting this project is a way of honoring their service and ensuring they can live in a dignified environment.
